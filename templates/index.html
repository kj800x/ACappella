!!Learning Tracks!!

... is a site where arrangers can upload their songs,
and singers can download learning tracks.

  • I'm an arranger
  • I'm a singer

Arrangers

Arrangers arrange the song that their vocal group is working on. This is the part they're experts in.

Once they know how their arrangement will sound, they will need to share it with their vocal group. They will probably notate it, and create learning tracks, which are audio files where a particular vocal part is isolated to make it easy to learn.

!!Learning Tracks!! takes care of mixing down individual versions of learning tracks so that arrangers just need to record individual parts and upload them.

Singers

Singers can then come to this website and create different mix-downs to suit their needs.

For example, a bass might come here and want to generate a version of a song his group is working on. He might decide to octavize the bass track and put it on the left channel. He can decide to put the lead and perc in the center, and to put the rest of voices on the right channel.

He then downloads the mix and puts it on his iPod or other MP3 player to listen and practice with.

Features

Creates a central place for the vocal group to find information.

Stores PDFs, elminating the problem of "I lost my sheet music".

Creates learning tracks from solo tracks of each part.
